Hotels & Resorts

Doreen Hotels and Resorts Ltd is the owning company of the luxurious 5 Star hotel property located in the premium Gulshan district of Dhaka operated by Starwood Hotels and Resorts Inc under the brand, Four Points by Sheraton Dhaka Gulshan. Phase 1 of the project, the Suites Annex has already in operation and handed over to Starwood since July 2014. Phase 2 of the project is the crown jewel of the real estate property holdings of DOREEN and is located in the heart of Gulshan Circle 2, the Doreen Tower.

The Four Points by Sheraton Dhaka Gulshan is a premium business hotel which will cater to the business travelers to Bangladesh with the primary focus in delivering comfortable rooms and personalized service.

The Suites Phase was designed to provide premium, personalized service to the top executives with large spacious suites, butler services, peaceful location and a seclude from a crowded environment, a home away from home in opulence and comfort. With 30 Suites accommodation, the Suites are designed for executives who have a panache for luxury. With two fine dining restaurants, rooftop swimming pool, fitness center, rooftop Japanese restaurant, the property in only about luxury and comfort.

The Tower Phase will provide more space, larger public areas, sky view rooftop fine dining and the pleasure for business travelers the view of Dhaka city and a comfortable stay. With 119 key rooms inventory, the Tower provides the business travelers to Dhaka with all the comfort and luxury in the heart of the Gulshan Circle 2. With larger facilities, the property provides the guests with more interaction with people and do business.

Both properties were developed with the highest international standards and has followed the strict design and safety standard guidelines internationally accepted and approved. Both properties were completely designed by reputed and best international designers and consultants.

The properties stand testament to the vision of DOREEN in building products of the highest international standards and a marquee to the direction where DOREEN is proceeding towards the future.
